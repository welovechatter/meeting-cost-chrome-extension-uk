Welcome to Calendar Meeting cost, a Chrome extension to see how much your meeting costs!

First set the average $ hourly rate for 1 employee (currently $)

References:

$50,000/year = $24/hour

$100,000/year = $48/hour

$200,000/year = $96/hour

Second, open Google Calendar and click on any event to see the $ meeting cost (at the top, under date/time).
